Capuccino

Cappuccino is a cup of milk coffee that originates from Italy and has been present in Europe and America since the 1980s. The taste of cappuccino coffee is generally very rich, even though it has been mixed with milk, the coffee taste is still strong.

Cappuccino is a typical Italian drink consisting of 1/3 espresso, 1/3 steamed milk and 1/3 milk froth.

Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a type of pasta that is long, thin, cylindrical, and dense, which, if you pay attention, resembles noodles in general. It is a staple in traditional Italian cooking. Because it is shaped like noodles, most people call it noodles from Italy, although many other pasta variants are shaped like noodles.

Like pasta in general, spaghetti is made from ground wheat and water. Italian spaghetti is made from durum wheat semolina, but elsewhere it can be made with other types of flour.
